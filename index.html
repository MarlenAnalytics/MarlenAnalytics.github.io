About Me

Hi! My name is Marlen and I earned my bachelor’s degree in physics and spent two years as a research intern, where I worked on calculations to further understand the internal structure of the neutron. During that time, I also worked shifts at Jefferson Lab’s accelerator facility and got hands-on with real-world data analysis from experiments run there. Since then, I’ve been working on improving my analytical skills, whether that’s cleaning messy datasets, exploring datasets with SQL and Python, or building visual stories in Tableau.
